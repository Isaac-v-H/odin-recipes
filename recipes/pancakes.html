Pancakes

description

Pancakes are easy to make, and delicious. They make a great breakfast, dinner or desert.

The following recipe is for 6 people

Ingridients

  • 1kg flower
  • 2l milk
  • 3 eggs
  • salt
  1. mix up the flower and milk untill fluid
  2. add the eggs and mix again
  3. add a pinch of salt
  4. bake the mixture into pancakes
