Snert

description

Snert is a great soup for when its cold

ingridients

  • 1 potato
  • 1 onion
  • 250g split peas
  • 150g carrot
  • 200g celeriac
  • 1 leak
  • 20g leaf celery
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1l water
  1. Put the split peas in a big pot with the water and let it boil for 30 minutes
  2. Chop the celeriac, potato, carrot, leak, onion, and leaf celery, and add them to the pot
  3. Add the bay leaf, and some salt and pepper. Boil for another 90 minutes.
  4. Leave for 24 hours
